> Generated by Stage 0 of `UI-ELEVATION-PLAYBOOK.md`. This is the checklist Stage 2 works against.

## вЪ†пЄП Cross-cutting finding (the #1 problem)
The design system is defined in `frontend/src/index.css` (CSS variables) + `tailwind.config.js`
(tokens), and the **shared classes** (`.btn-primary`, `.input-field`, `.data-table`, `.badge`,
`.modal-panel`, etc.) are excellent. But **most pages bypass tokens with hardcoded hex values in
inline `style={{}}` props** вАФ `border: '1px solid #23252a'`, `#34343a`, `#30363d`, `#c9d1d9`,
`rgba(63,185,80,вА¶)`, `rgba(248,81,73,вА¶)`. 164 such occurrences across 19 files.

Consequence: the **light theme is effectively broken** (hardcoded *dark* hairlines render as dark
lines on a light canvas), and the design language is inconsistent. Fixing this is the spine of
Stage 2: replace every hardcoded token with a CSS variable.

Additional cross-cutting gaps:
- `var(--font-mono)` is referenced in 5 files but **never defined** in `:root` вЖТ falls back to
  inherited font, not JetBrains Mono.
- `prefers-reduced-motion` is handled; `::selection` and `theme-color` meta are **not** styled.
- focus-visible rings exist on `.btn-*` / `.input-field` but **missing** on custom checkbox/toggle
  and custom inline icon buttons.
